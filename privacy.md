# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-19

## 1. Introduction

This policy describes how PUBG Stats Bot ("the Bot") handles user data.

## 2. Data We Collect

The Bot processes the following data when you invoke a command:

- Discord user locale (to determine response language)
- Command inputs you provide (e.g., player names)

## 3. Data We Do Not Collect

- We do not store Discord messages or message content.
- We do not store user IDs, server IDs, or any personally identifiable information.
- We do not track usage analytics or telemetry.

## 4. Third-Party Services

The Bot interacts with the following external services:

- **Discord API** — to receive commands and send responses. Subject to Discord's [Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy).
- **PUBG API** — to retrieve player statistics. Subject to KRAFTON's privacy policies.

## 5. Data Retention

The Bot may temporarily cache player statistics in memory to reduce API calls. Cached data is not persisted to disk and is lost when the Bot restarts.

## 6. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 7. Changes

We may update this policy at any time. Changes take effect immediately upon publication in this repository.

## 8. Contact

For questions about this policy, open an issue in the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/theodorosidmar/pubg-stats-bot).
